What does "Engineering Science" even mean?

This is a program within UC Berkeley's College of Engineering. There are four interdisciplinary majors: each one trains the student in both an engineering skillset for problem-solving and a scientific mindset for theory and laboratory. Each major is flexible to fit the student's academic interests across departments.

What do students do after graduation?

About 75% of students enter graduate school, mostly in physics and engineering. The rest generally enter the workforce in various jobs in science, technology, and consulting.
